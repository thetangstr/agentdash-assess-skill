## What We Do

We assess organizations for AI agent adoption readiness and deliver a crystallized spec: a concrete pilot plan with go/no-go reasoning, agent architecture, success metrics, and a 6-week rollout.

## Why This Exists

Most AI agent initiatives fail not because of technology — but because of unclear requirements. Nobody defined what "success" means. Nobody named the systems that need to integrate. Nobody established error tolerance or escalation paths.

Our assessment applies mathematical clarity gates before any execution begins. The output is a project charter, not a strategy deck.

## The Engagement

| Phase | Duration | What happens |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| **Intake** | 10 min | Company context, goals, pain points |
| **Research** | 15 min | Website analysis, industry detection |
| **Deep Interview** | 45 min | Socratic requirements gathering |
| **Report** | Same day | Crystallized spec + PDF |

Total time commitment from client: **~60 minutes**

## Output

Every assessment produces:

1. **Readiness verdict** — Is your organization ready for agents? What scope is the right wedge?
2. **Agent architecture** — Tier 1-5 classification, build/buy calls per layer
3. **Closed-loop design** — How the agent measures success, learns, and updates
4. **6-week pilot plan** — Week-by-week milestones with success metric checkpoints
5. **Go/No-Go reasoning** — Why now or why not yet
